Camera control.

"W" - go forward
"S" - go backward
"A" - go left
"D" - go right
"↑" - turn camera up
"↓" - turn camera down
"←" - turn camera left
"→" - turn camera right

Elevator control.

"1" - move elevator to the Nth floor (specify floor in console)
"2" - call elevator to floor where camera is located
"3" - open/close doors
"4" - turn engine on/off (if engine is off, elevator does not function)
